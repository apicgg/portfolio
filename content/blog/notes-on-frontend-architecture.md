---
title: Notes on Frontend Architecture
date: 2026-02-18
summary: The systems I trust most are the ones that stay boring under pressure.
---

Frontend architecture is less about fashionable tools and more about pressure management.

The questions I keep coming back to are simple:

- Can a new engineer understand the data flow quickly?
- Can the UI change without rewriting half the app?
- Can we debug production issues without guesswork?

I prefer architectures that preserve these properties even when product scope expands. The goal is not maximal flexibility. The goal is controlled change.

Good structure becomes visible when deadlines get tight. Bad structure becomes visible even faster.

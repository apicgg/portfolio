---
title: What I Want From a Portfolio
date: 2026-01-12
summary: A portfolio should explain taste, judgment, and direction, not just list tools.
---

A portfolio does not need to behave like a company website.

It should answer a narrower set of questions:

- What kind of work do I care about?
- What technical decisions do I make repeatedly?
- What standards do I hold when building products?

Projects matter, but writing matters too. Writing exposes how you think when there is no design polish or demo video to hide behind.

That is why I wanted a dedicated writing section here instead of burying everything inside the homepage.
